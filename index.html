About Me

Born and raised in the Pacific Northwest, I have grown up around technology from a young age. As a budding technologist I am continualy facinated by full stack development and its ability to use both the logic and creative side of my brain. I hope to grow into a software engineering role as I look for my first job in coding.

Background

Graduating in 2014 from the University of St. Andrews in Scotland, I began my career interested in political policy. Taking this interest to political campaigns and eventually a congressman's district office, in the summer of 2015 my father fell ill and passed, which put a pause on my career. I spent the next year helping to close down his small business where I learned basic book keeping and client managment. I then spent two years working odd jobs while I took time to think about where I wanted to go next. Remembering my initial interest in computer programming I eventually decided on coding and have since devoted my energy towards landing a software engineering job.
